## 关于这款游戏
  
This is a unofficial derivative fangame of the Touhou Project. It complies with the official guidelines for derivative work.

### Features（特点）
  
Learn the basics of Cognitive Behavioral Therapy with Yuuka Kazami in this cute visual novel!  

Learn scientifically proven techniques to help manage your mental health problems!  

Cute art!  

Atmospheric Scenery!  

Easy going, casual visual novel gameplay!  

Engaging Dialogue!  

在这个可爱的视觉小说中，与风见幽香一起学习CBT的基础知识，学习经过科学验证的技术来帮助解决您的心理健康问题！  

可爱的画风！美丽的风景！生动的对话!轻松，休闲的视觉小说游戏！
