<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">An obnoxious, rude sort of creature.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">邪魅是一种令人厌恶的粗俗无礼的生物。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A mountain youkai that managed to get locked up for wandering into the Expanse.
