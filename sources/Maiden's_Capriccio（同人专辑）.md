## 评论
  
Touhou 8's soundtrack is probably one of the most recognizable 2hu music right after EoSD! Reimu's Theme instantly caught my attention as soon as I heard it, it's such a strong and amazingly composed piece by ZUN! I hope you guys enjoy my take on it!
I appreciate everyone's patience as I've put this song together. Last month was pretty rough for me, there was a lot of things going on but I pulled through! I feel like I'm making bigger improvements with sound quality and arrangement. I'm feeling more comfortable with dareful approaches and experimenting with different drums, guitar tones and even video effects. Really proud how this song and its video came out! Let me know what you think!&#160;:)
  
  
In Touhou IN Reimu and Marisa battle each other, so I thought it'd be a nice move to reference Love-colored Master Spark (恋色マスタースパーク) right in the middle of the song. They fit so well together too!
  
  
Enjoy the video! Thank you so much!
