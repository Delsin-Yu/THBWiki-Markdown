## 评论

  
Devout followers of the Broken Moon, We have Returned  

AlterSynth presents: TAHWA 4  

  

The signature middle eastern/arabia electronic Touhou music arrangement album is back for its fourth rendition. While the holy month has since passed, it is never not the time for celebration.  

  

TAHWA 4 is a monstrous 13-track album that is spearheaded by AlterSynth's resident producers, as well as plenty of guests, fresh and familiar. All tracks in this album are unique in that none of the original pieces have been rearranged and put in a TAHWA album previously.
