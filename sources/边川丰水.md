Before Marisa found her, she was doing this a lot.<br>However, the catch is that when she does this, not all of the energy goes to the target, since otherwise, the target would likely die, or at least be irreversibly damaged.<br><br>Instead, this energy just gets expelled into Gensokyo's atmosphere. The issue with this is that it was pretty much the same thing Rachel's invention was doing.<br>In other words, if Housui was left unchecked, then Gensokyo's border would've been destroyed anyways as if the incident was never resolved.<br><br>...not that Housui would've ever realized this, nor would she have thought it was her fault if it was to happen.<br><br>Due to being a rather recent youkai, she doesn't quite understand everything yet. In fact, she was quite confused by pretty much all of her surroundings at first.<br>She didn't even quite understand what it was she was doing to all the fairies and youkai passing through the Amaryllis field, she just thought it was cool.<br><br>After Marisa's fight, the two of them paired up with the purpose of exploring Housui's ability more. Marisa wound up deducing that her abilities were able to make people stronger, and began collaborating with her frequently for her magic experiments.<br><br>In general, Housui and Marisa wound up really hitting it off, and wound up becoming great friends after their fight.<br><br>Rachel also frequently attempts to experiment on Housui, but Housui always refuses to do it and is rather annoyed at how frequently Rachel pesters her over this.<br></div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem"></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
