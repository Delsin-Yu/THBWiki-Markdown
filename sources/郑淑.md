## 设定

### 东方天逆袭

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方天逆袭-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5929\u9006\u88ad&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A divine beast from the mainland who drifts about on Youkai Mountain's plateaus.<br><br>The kirin are a species that bear a strong sense of justice that, when combined with their supernatural ability to sense the wickedness in humans, makes them a valued asset of the Ministry of Right and Wrong: it's not uncommon to find kirin working Part-time or even full-time jobs as assistants for the Yama.<br><br>They're benevolent towards those who are pure of heart, and absolutely merciless towards the wicked.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">她是从大陆而来的神兽麒麟。最近在妖怪之山上行动。<br><br>麒麟们是持有很强烈的正义感的神兽。因为在人们和他们呆在一起的时候身带的邪恶能够被麒麟感知到，所以他们在是非曲直厅占有很重要的地位。在这里到处都是给阎魔提供临时和长期工作帮助的麒麟。<br><br>对内心纯洁的人来说，他们是仁慈的，但是打击邪恶势力时他们毫不留情。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方天逆袭-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5929\u9006\u88ad&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">On occasion, Shu will travel around to various places across Gensokyo and play soothing songs on he erhu. Anyone who hears the music from her erhu will find themselves eased by its sound, and any feelings of rage or hostility within their heart will be instantly dissipated.<br>This music is also said to be able to calm violent storms and pacify vengeful spirits.<br><br>This time, at the request of the Yamaxanadu, she and several other kirin had been working on doing damage control on the incident in Gensokyo. In her case, she was to make full use of her abilities for the sake of undoing the distortions the arrows caused, and preventing any violent outbreaks. <br>Through, while she was aware that the incident had come from Youkai Mountain, she was uncertain as to the exact nature of the mastermind, nor what their intentions were.<br><br>At any rate, she figured that some sort of emergency was occurring there, so once she made out a group of humans and youkai traversing the haze-covered False Heaven Shelf, her kirin instincts kicked in and she felt obligated to drive these girls back, since she felt they'd be in danger(or in Sejia's case, because she felt her to be a danger in of itself.).<br><br>In the end, Shu had discovered that not only were the girls stronger than she initially assumed, but that their goals aligned with hers, thus she allowed them to travel further up the mountain, while also monitoring them from a distance.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">有时，郑淑会在幻想乡上空的各个地方用她的二胡演奏抚慰人心的音乐。只要人们听到她的二胡演奏出的音乐，就会感到放松，他们心中的恶意也随之马上烟消云散。这样的音乐据说还有使暴怒的怨灵平息愤懑的能力。<br><br>最近，在阎魔的要求下，郑淑和别的一些麒麟要去幻想乡控制异变造成的伤害。于是她全力以赴地阻止奇怪的箭矢引发的暴力事件，以免对人们造成更多伤害。<br><br>后来，她意识到导致这场异变的原因来自妖怪之山，但是她始终不清楚异变的始作俑者是谁，以及他们要干什么。<br><br>无论如何，她觉得那里肯定发生了很紧急的情况。她派出一群人穿过雾霾笼罩的伪天棚，同时她的天性提醒着她，自己有义务把那些朝着妖怪之山前进的女孩们带回去，不然她们会经历危险（正邪除外，因为郑淑觉得她本身就是个危险分子）。<br><br>后来，郑淑发现她们比她最初设想的要更加强大，且与她志同道合，于是准许她们向山里继续行进，同时时刻监视着她们的行踪。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方天逆袭-3" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5929\u9006\u88ad&quot;,3&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">(PS. The nebulous familiar she's riding on is known as "Kumolopard". As his name suggests, he's a cloud-like entity that looks exceedingly like a giraffe.<br>He's brimming with curiosity, but his innocuous appearance belies his true strength; Once he perceives a threat, he's able to quickly dispatch it by breathing holy fire from his mouth.).</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">注：她乘坐的云形使魔叫做“库莫罗珀”，正如其名是酷似长颈鹿的云一样的存在。<br><br>它有着旺盛的好奇心，但是和无害的样子不同，它拥有察觉威胁时从口中吐出圣火使之迅速退散的强大力量。<br><br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
