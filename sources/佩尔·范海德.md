### 东方眠世界设定

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Native to the Outside World, she came to Gensokyo alongside the Moriya when they move in. So for the second time since she gain consciousness, she went to and over the mountain.<br>It is there, high up in the sky, that she met a very impatient human girl...</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">当天空破碎之时，她对此很是好奇，想凑近些看个仔细。因此，自她获得意识后的第二次，她靠近并飞越了妖怪之山。<br>也就是在那里，在高天之上，她遇到了一位很不耐烦的人类少女…<br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
