## 评论
4. Chained Oni bound to Mt. Ooe

  
Playful and strong. Being an oni seems to be fun, a carefree life full of feasts and joy. And drinking.   

  

10. Sacrifice for Vlad Tepes' Descendant（Septette for the Dead Princess）

  
The alternate title is for the case no one gets confused.   

I arranged a second piece to her theme with different instruments, but I´m still working on it.    

It will be finished in one/two days, so stay tuned! :P   

  

11. The Sacred Moon Battle

  
Even for the original, it is an extremely energetic Stage 1 Theme. Quite promising and catching, don´t you think?   

  
  
There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as followed:   

"According to the legend of Kaguya-hime, a secret civilization is supposed to exist on the moon, that waged war against an army of youkai several thousand years ago.   

If you undertake this, you could assume that something must have happened at the moon landing fourty-one years back.   

At least if this civilization really exists."   

  

14. Revolutinary Festival of Magical Girls（The Centennial Festival For Magical Girls）

  
Wee, my favourite extra stage theme. 
Although it didn´t turn out "festivalish", I like it nonetheless.   

  

16. Iris -Serenity-

  
As always, the ending theme is quite setting, calm and relaxing. It is my favourite PC-98 ending theme closely before Eternal Full Moon.   

  
  
There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as followed:   

"My professor told me that an acquintance of his knows a girl who is also interested in the supernatural.   

Her name is Maribel Han and she´s living in Kyoto.   

When I get time, I will ask Akina if she eventually joins me on my trip to said town.   

Without a doubt, Maribel is going to help us a lot on our researches."   

  

18. Elegance of Purest Nature

  
Probably ZUN most melancholy theme besides Green-Eyed Jealousy or Vanishing Dream ~ Lost Dream.   

  
  
There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as followed:   

"Winter soon is going to end and cherry trees will start to bloom again. It is a mesmirizing time, the change of seasons. It shows us that nothing is eternal, but not gone forever, even if it isn´t present anymore. It will go on, because time is endless.    

Akina and I will continue our research until we found reasonable answers to our questions. But for now I´ll enjoy the sunset, alongside a cup of tea...~"
