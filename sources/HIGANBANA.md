## 评论

  
Honored guests and heathens, we are taking over Hell.
  
  
AlterSynth presents: HIGANBANA, a Touhou Juuouen - Unfinished Dream of All Living Ghost - arrangement album. Nine tracks from the latest entry in the timeworn Touhou series, all remixed and rearranged into the tried and true electronic sounds of AlterSynth.
  
  
HIGANBANA features works from all of AlterSynth's core members spread across two sides.「Lobelia」is emotional, bright and somber at the same time, while「Lycoris」highlights the darker, rougher aspect of AlterSynth's production. All core AlterSynth members are present in this album, along with good friends Paraguna Satsuki X and typeMARS bringing in their hard-hitting kicks and basslines. Also accompanying the album are the commanding vocal works of Arsa Rawindra and Xander Walherich, as well as lyrical work from Hisuikouha.The illustrious cover art of HIGANBANA is provided by long-time friend kokuya and much of the design are provided by our latest newcomer nyx.xcf.
