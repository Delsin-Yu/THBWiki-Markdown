### 新版设定

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="新版设定-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u65b0\u7248\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Crinkum-Crankum Feminal Scientist<br><br>レイチェル・ミケランジェロ<br>Rachel Michelangelo<br><br>Species：Human<br>Ability：Capable of manipulating atoms and matter<br><br>Occasionally, portals will show up in the outside world that wind up bringing people to Gensokyo, mostly against their will. Rachel was one such victim of these portals.<br><br>Before getting teleported to Gensokyo, Rachel held a special interest in science.<br>She loved taking things apart to study what made them work, she loved combining various substances to witness the chemical reaction, and most importantly she just simply loved experimenting.<br><br>She was rather close to her family, and could be seen with at least one fellow family member quite frequently.<br>This made her arrival in Gensokyo all the more painful for her, since it wound up tearing her away from them.<br><br>In addition, she was attacked by a rather violent shadow youkai shortly upon entering, and while she came out of the experience unscathed somehow, it still traumatized her and shaped her opinion of Gensokyo.<br><br>According to her, Gensokyo is just a place where weirdos lie.<br>According to her, Gensokyo is just a place where a predator can attack at every corner.<br><br>According to her, Gensokyo is an evil monster that tore her away from the ones she loved.<br><br>She eventually found refuge in a cave, and started constructing her own lab there. She initially began with just some simple experiments, before constructing the end goal of making an invention.<br>Said invention would destroy the barrier that was preventing her from coming home.<br><br>She also didn't like the idea of magic, since it contradicted everything she knew before, and was also mainly used by those that attacked her.<br>She's still adamant about saying her ability is just a result of her being that good at science, and not something that's due to magic.<br><br>She began hiring people to help her work on the invention.<br>She also tried hiring people to experiment on, but nobody actually wanted to be experimented on for free, which led to her kidnapping people to use as lab rats instead.<br>She stopped after Unk began haunting her after his death, though.<br><br>Most of her employees soon took notice of the fact that she wasn't a perfectly sane individual.<br>She made her warped view of Gensokyo apparent quite frequently.<br>She contradicted her own words all the time.<br>The only employee she made it clear that she actually cared about was Jun, and she treated all the others like dirt.<br><br>Most importantly, she clearly had no respect for others' lives, being apathetic if one of her employees died in an accident or something.<br><br>In fact, the only thing she really cared about was just returning home, something which soon turned into an obsession that she let take control over her.<br>Most people besides Jun simply viewed her as a lost cause, and by the time she finished her invention Jun was the only employee, and honestly, only friend, that she still had.<br><br>If it wasn't for Reimu's fight with her, she likely would've destroyed the border and killed most of Gensokyo's residents without a thought.<br><br>After the incident, she wound up not caring about getting home as much, and learned to show more compassion towards others, though she still gets easily angered.<br><br>Most of all though, she always enjoys a good fight.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem"></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
