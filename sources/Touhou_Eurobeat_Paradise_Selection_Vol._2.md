## 评论

  
The second volume of the series Touhou Eurobeat Paradise Selection.  

  

This album contains eurobeat arrangements of songs from the Touhou Project (Touhou - The Embodiment of Scarlet Devil, Touhou - Perfect Cherry Blossom.)
