2. Train showing me my past（Departure of Eastern Journey『2』）

  
...and she is always late.   

There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as followed:   

  
  
Maribel quickly ran through the streets of Kyoto, which were surprisingly empty. She was searching for something... or someone. Flickering lights briefly illuminated her way, leading her to her destination. The subway.   

"Renko!", Maribel yelled.    

Renko, who was standing next to the store located at this restless place, fastly turned her head into the direction of Maribel´s voice.   

M: "Renko, I told you we shouldn´t go that early!"    

Renko took a bite of the food she just bought and responded with a playful smile on her face.   

R: "Aw, come on. Isn´t it more fun to go on a journey when it´s midnight? It´s more romantic, and you do like that kind of stuff, don´t you?"   

M: "That´s not the point! I just had to hurry, because you said I should be at the subway in fifteen minutes! I weren´t able to do my hair..."

4. Wooden Heart（Forest of Eastern Journey 『4』）

  
This forest is tremendous! It reminds me of the one in Aokigahara.   

  
  
There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as following:   

  
  
As the two members of the Ghostly Field Club entered the forest that seperated town and subway, their way got blocked by an overthrown treetrunk lying across the street.    

M: "Oh my, who would´ve thought that?" Maribel examinated trunk and street, trying to find a way to be still able to transport the carriage.   

R: "Maybe if you pass the tree we could lift the carriage over it?"   

M: "I´m not pretty sure if that works, but I´ll give it try."   

Renko heaved it up, whereas Maribel was pulling it from the other side. Maybe a little bit too hard, since the carriage fell over the try and almost hit its counterpart.    

R: "Maribel, everything alright?"   

M: "Yeah, I´m fine, it seems the former user of our now broken transportation means forgot to take his money with him."   

  

5. Mystery of mankind（Saga of Eastern Journey 『5』）

  
Why does she need a map?   

  
  
There is a story synopsis to this song that goes as following:   

  
  
"Maybe it wasn´t that bad we took the carriage with us.", said Maribel as she was observing an old shrine, which seemed to function as some kind of gate.   

Several traditionally japanese constructed houses were piercing the sky and a great wall elegantly surrounded them, but still stayed as strong and unbreakable as it was meant to be.   

"I told you it gives us a use. Even though I weren´t expecting that.", responded Renko, who was walking out of a store situated along the huge road, connecting the building alongside it.    

M: "Too bad, we do not know where we are."   

R: "Do you think this map could help us? It´s kind of tricky to read."   

M: "Of course, you´re holding it the wrong way."
