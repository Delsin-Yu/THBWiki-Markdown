## 评论
  
I made a guitar cover of Parsee's Theme from Touhou SA! It's been requested quite a few times by you lads, so here it is! It's always been a very unique 2hu song, but I think Parsee deserves a lot more attention (Pun not intended lol). I hope you like my take on the song - Let me know what you think! 😄
  
  
I also played parts of Sakuya's Theme (Flowering Night) and Orin's Stage Theme (Lullaby of Deserted Hell).
