## 评论

  
鬼人正邪は地底へ来ました！そして、全てをひっくり返す気を持って来ました。かわいそうなパルスィはその中にめり込んでしまいます。  

  

___

  
Reversal magic devil Seija Kijin has a quest - to recruit as many followers as possible to her cause of overthrowing the tyranny of the strong! She sees a good candidate in the lethally bitter Parsee, but her pigheadedness is to much even for Seija to handle, so she smacks Parsee with her magical mallet - which causes a complete reversal of the personality of the avatar of Jealousy. But an emotion-based youkai that doesn't maintain her source of power might die! Can she be saved in time, and maybe learn something along the way?
