## 评论
1. Nostalgic Rain

  
Loose Rain (or Lose Rain if you want) paralyzed me the first time I heard it. I was so excited about the Extra Boss that I died a few seconds after the stage began.   

It really is an outstanding track.   

  

2. when WINTER ends

  
Caught in an endless cycle. When something dies, something new has to take its place.   

  
  
I always thought Letty´s theme was somehow aggresive and threathing, as if she´s attacking you with her last remaining power and energy before she leaves and takes winter with her.   

  

3. Windless Girl

  
Words are relative, since the truth is unchangeable.   

  
  
I always enjoyed StB´s soundtrack more than DS´s, but I do not know why. Maybe it´s because DS was easier, so that the tracks couldn´t grow on to me? Nah, I´m not sure.. ^^"   

Anyways, Nemesis´ Stronghold fits surprisingly well to Sleepless Night, although their sound character isn´t that similar.   

  

4. Kerzenschein

  
Sometimes, even a shrine maiden prays at her own shrine.   

  
  
Too bad ZUN did not include this wonderful track. Perhaps he wants to use it in a different game? We will see.   

  

5. Lunar Research Brigade

  
Having fled from the place she loves the most, her life became almost senseless.   

  
  
Woo, new things are fun! It´s interesting to experiment with music genres and gives me a completely new insight into the latter mentioned.   

  

6. Lovely Express of Hemisphere

  
This express sure is mysterious... Perfect for me, isn´t it?   

  
  
Oh my, I wish every ride would be so relaxing as portrayed in the illustration. Unfortunately, it´s not.&#160;:/   

  
  
All that urgency, rush and constant hustle is truly a pity. It´s like everyone wants to escape it´s life by quickly running from point A to point B. However, they seem to forget that they are caught in an endless cycle -- which is life.    

Kind of funny when you think of the usual train station, which are clear, widely open and easily accessible. In my country, the train stations are quite winding, so I often get lost. To my own surprise, it happens to me even in my hometown (which is embarrassingly the capital at the same time...)!    

  
  
Hehe, I guess I´m just that kind of person who loses himself in thoughts permanently.   

  

7. Sweet Maiden Doll

  
Oh my, this atmosphere is rather trembling.   

  
  
It has lighthearted feel to it, doesn´t it? Couldn´t find a fitting picture, so I chose Medicine.   

  

8. Darkest Phoenix Flight

  
Living at the place she hates the most, her mind is dangerously instable.   

  
  
Er...I think arranging Immortal Smoke is rather hard, isn´t it? I wasn´t sure about the use of instruments. Anyway, another arrangement will follow shortly. I think you can guess what it is, right?&#160;;)   

  

9. voyage of burning stars

  
"So this is the place?" "Yes, everything started here."   

  
  
Oh my, I have to say the original is quite dark and obscure for the Three Fairies. It sounds chasing, as if something sinister is running after you, but you cannot see it, let alone estimate what it is. Overall it gives a tense, but also frightening feeling. I would suggest you to listen to the original.   

  

10. Narrowing Ice（Tranquilizing Ice）

  
I´m not so content about this one. Maybe the piano was too hard?   

  

11. Tree-Ocean of Hourai

12. Celestial Ruin

  
Whoo, accordions are awesome.   

And Sky Ruin is better than Traditional Old Man.   

  

13. Fanfare des melancholischen Weiß

14. Kappa's New Invention
