## 评论
  
It's finally here! You guys have suggested a cover of Pure Furies many times, and I'm super proud to finally deliver it to you bros!
Junko's theme is one of my all time favorite songs from Touhou. I always loved the 80s vibes it had, instantly followed by heavy guitars! That part was probably the hardest to play, since it's many fast passages just for rhythm guitar alone! I've put a lot of thought into every single aspect of the music and the video turned out looking a lot sharper than expected too. The original composition of Pure Furies is mostly just a few motifs, so I wanted to diversify the ideas as much as possible. There's two unique clean guitar parts, a cinematic build-up, piano and a guitar solo too! Let me know what you think!
  
  
I've referenced Pandemonic Planet (Hecatia's Theme) and Pierrot of the Star-Spangled Banner (Clownpiece's Theme), because them and Junko are obviously the iconic moon trio! I also wore the Weirdo T-Shirt in the video haha!
Hope you enjoy! Thank you so much!
