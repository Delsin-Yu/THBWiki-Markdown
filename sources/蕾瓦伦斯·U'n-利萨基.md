## 设定

### 东方眠世界设定

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">In the beginning, around 13.8 billion years ago, a god created the known universe we live in.<br>This god was a very special one, a kotoamatsukami, one of the first to ever exist.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">起初，在大约138亿年<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">3</a></sup>前，一位神明创造了我们所居住的宇宙。<br>这位神明是非常特别的：一名别天神，最初的存在者之一。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Throughout History, different religions gave a lot of different names to the concept of creation.<br>But her one and only name is Reverence U'n-Llisardggy.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">纵观历史，不同的宗教给“创世神”的概念冠以了许多不同的名字。<br>不过她唯一的名字是蕾瓦伦斯·U'n-利萨基。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-3" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,3&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Humans like to think everything was created for them, or that it was at least actively made by this greater force.<br>It is not so.<br>Reverence creates universes inside bubbles, floating in a nameless, infinite, non-Euclidean space.<br>But her involvement stops there -- she doesn't know what's inside the bubble she blows unless she goes inside. Everything happening in them is just a<br>consequence of whatever laws of physics the places inside follow.<br>Bubbles will sometimes regroup together into clusters, linking those worlds directly. For example, the Outside World, the Dream World and Gensokyo are all located in separate bubbles, but since they are part of the same cluster, one can "easily" go from one to another.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">人类总是喜欢设想一切事物都是为他们而创造的，或者至少一切都是这种伟力的有意创造。<br>然而并非如此。<br>蕾瓦伦斯创造出泡泡中的宇宙，而那些泡泡漂浮在一个无名、无尽的非欧几里得空间之中。<br>但她的参与止步于此——她并不知道自己吹出的泡泡中有何物，除非她进入其中。气泡中发生的一切，<br>都是其内部自有的物理定律产生的结果。<br>泡泡有时会聚集在一起成为集群，将其中的世界链接在一起。例如说，外界，梦世界和幻想乡都位于各自的泡泡之中，<br>但因为它们处于同一个集群，所以可以“轻易”地在它们之间往来。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-4" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,4&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A few hundred years ago, someone managed to leave the bubble they were living in on their own.<br>This someone was a human-looking lady with creepy eyes. <br>The lady came to ask Reverence to create a world just for her. She obviously refused, but said she won't stop her from trying herself.<br>That is how Reverence has always been -- she never gets involved in anything. She is merely a spectator of her own creations.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">在数百年前，某人成功凭借自己的力量离开了所生存的泡泡。<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">4</a></sup><br>这是一位貌似人类的，有着悚人眼瞳的女子。<br>这位女子要求蕾瓦伦斯为她创造一个世界。蕾瓦伦斯理所当然地拒绝了，但也说她并不会阻止女子自行尝试。<br>这就是蕾瓦伦斯的一贯作风——她不会参与任何事。她仅仅只是她创造物的观察者罢了。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-5" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,5&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">The lady came and went, and tried to create a world of her own multiple times.<br>One day, she succeeded, much to Reverence's surprise.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">这位女子往而复来，几次地尝试创造一个自己的世界。<br>有一天，出乎蕾瓦伦斯的意料，她终于成功了。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-6" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,6&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A long, long time before those events, she would spend a lot of time visiting the worlds she created.<br>During that time, she went through billions of different places and saw billions of different forms of life.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">在这些事情发生前的很久，她会花上许多时间拜访她所创造的诸世界。<br>在那段日子里，她历行无数地界，遍阅了亿万种生命形态。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-7" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,7&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">But one day, she stumbled upon a little rock. It was floating in the middle of space, covered with water and life.<br>It was Earth... but not really.<br>It was extremely similar to our planet: it had humans, societies and religions, but it was not the planet Earth we know.<br>On it, she discovered things she had never seen before. Hatred, wars, violence. An unimaginable amount of pain emanated from this single rock.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">但是有一天，她驻足于一颗小小的石子。它漂浮在宇宙之中，为水与生命所覆盖。<br>那就是地球…不过不完全是。<br>它与我们所居的星球非常相似，存在着人类，也有社会和宗教，但它并不是我们所知的地球。<br>在这颗星球上，她发现了她从未有见的事物。仇恨、战争、暴力。这颗小小的石子上诞生了难以想象、不可尽数的痛苦。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-8" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,8&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">She couldn't accept this.<br>She couldn't accept that she had created, even indirectly, such imperfect creatures.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">她无法接受。<br>她无法接受——即使并非直接地——她创造了如此不完美的生灵。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-9" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,9&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">So, for the first time, she decided to interfere with one of her creations.<br>She reached toward a random human, a young girl, and asked her to solve all of humanity's misdeeds, for she would destroy everything if she were to fail.<br>Well aware that this human couldn't just "do it", she gifted to her the power to do so.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">因此，史无前例地，她决定向她的造物中的一种加以干涉。<br>她随机地找上了一位女性——那是一个少女——并请她去解决全人类的种种罪行，而若她失败，蕾瓦伦斯就将毁灭一切。<br>蕾瓦伦斯很清楚这个人类并不能轻松地做到这些，因而赋予了她成事所需要的力量。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-10" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,10&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">The next time the two of them met, the young girl and her pets were alone on a raft, floating over the girl's submerged universe.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">而二人再见面时，女孩与她的宠物一起乘着筏子，孑然一身地漂浮在她被淹没的宇宙之上。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-11" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,11&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Animals, plants, rocks, suns, stray molecules floating in space. For the god, it was like losing trillions upon trillions of children.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">动物、植物、岩球、恒星，还有漂浮在宇宙之中的零散的分子。对于蕾瓦伦斯来说，它们都是同一的：生命。她能听见每一个原子的心绪。<br>她认为存在者皆应存在，她爱着一切存在的事物。<br>但此刻，她什么都听不见了。<br>整个世界，这个泡泡内部的一切，都死亡了。对于神来说，这就宛如失去了亿万的孩子。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-12" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,12&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">And it was all because of Reverence's god complex.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">而这全归咎于蕾瓦伦斯的万能神力。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-13" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,13&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Ashamed of her actions, she took the human girl with her, popped the lifeless bubble, and promised that she would never go inside another one of her <br>creations ever again.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">愧疚难当，她将人类女孩带在身边，毁灭了那个了无生命的泡泡，<br>然后发誓她再也不会进入她的造物之中。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-14" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,14&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Considering Nua as her "mistake", she tries to make up by helping her find a new home.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">因为她觉得曾对努亚犯下了错误，所以她也期望帮努亚找到一个新家，以此来弥补自己的过失。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方眠世界设定-15" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u7720\u4e16\u754c\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,15&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Sometimes, she stares at the infinite nothingness of the nameless place, and wonders if all of this is somehow contained in another, bigger bubble...</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">有时，她的目光会停驻在那片无名的空间中的，无穷无尽的虚无，她想，这一切是否也处在一个更大的泡泡中呢……<br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
