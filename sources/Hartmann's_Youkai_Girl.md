## 评论
  
This song has been requested a lot! Koishi's theme is such an eerie special track. It's very similar to Clownpiece's Theme or Heian Alien, with its strong dissonances and chaotic feel. I really wanted to bring that forward by adding choir and full orchestra to support the guitars. This is my first time writing huge vocal parts like this, I hope you like it! I'm slowly mastering my music-making to reach the quality I've always loved and each new cover makes it a step closer. I hope you like my take on this classic Touhou banger. Let me know what you think! Thanks for listening!
  
  
I've also referenced Satori Maiden ~ 3rd Eye (Satori's Theme) after the guitar solo! The sisters need to stay together. It's definitely one of my favorite songs from Touhou SA.
