### 东方实在相

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方实在相-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5b9e\u5728\u76f8&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Somehow, dream logic added the knowledge of a disease-causing particle called a virus to the inhabitants of Gensokyo. This, combined with an increased rate of youkai birth, resulted in Acytota, a youkai virus.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">通过某种方式，梦逻辑将一种名为病毒的致病微粒的知识赋予了幻想乡的居民。有了这份知识的存在，加之以妖怪诞生率的增长，艾科托塔，一只病毒妖怪，就这样产生了。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方实在相-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5b9e\u5728\u76f8&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Acytota's reason for being as a youkai is straightforward: create more viruses. She can't do this by herself, having to absorb energy from other living beings to assemble them. She'll seize without a second thought any opportunity that crosses her path to get close enough to others to steal the required energy. Effortlessly matching her tactics to specific people and situations, she can appear to have a completely different personality from one moment to the next.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">作为妖怪，艾科托塔存在的意义很简单：让病毒增殖。她并不能独力完成此事，因为要组装病毒，她必须从别的生物身上汲取能量。因此，一旦机会来临，她就会不假思索地接近他人，来窃取所需的能量。根据具体的对象与情形，她能轻松地拟定相应的对策，为此，她的个性也可能瞬息万变。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方实在相-3" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5b9e\u5728\u76f8&quot;,3&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Despite these devious machinations, it appears that she isn't terribly successful at creating viruses. Perhaps her strategies need more work, or maybe she's unconsciously holding herself back because of that thing where dream logic's made people nicer? Gosh, there's a lot to take into account.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">不过，尽管如此诡计多端，她在制造病毒上却不是很成功。也许是因为她的策略需要改进，又或者是她受到梦逻辑使人从善的影响而不自觉地克制了自己？啊呀，要考虑的因素很多呢。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方实在相-4" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u5b9e\u5728\u76f8&quot;,4&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Over the course of the dream logic incident, Acytota was outwitted (and outgunned) by all of her potential victims, leading to a sense of existential disappointment. What was the point of dream logic bringing her into existence if she couldn't fulfill her reason for being? Aya explained to her, though, that Gensokyo is meant to be a place where humans and youkai can live in a symbiotic relationship. Everyone must learn to channel their desires in a way that preserves that balance. Acytota didn't need to create as many viruses as possible; she just needed some kind of adversarial relationship with humans. Even if the "adversarial" part was something as casual and safe as fighting with danmaku or giving people a good, harmless scare. Or, as Katerina suggested, warning them about the importance of maintaining good health habits. Katerina turned to Kosuzu and her library of forbidden information to find something that might help Acytota peacefully settle into life in Gensokyo, and Acytota promised to try her best to be a good citizen.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">在梦逻辑异变中，艾科托塔被她所有潜在的受害者都打败了（智力上和武力上都是），这让她开始有些自我怀疑。如果她甚至不能实现自己存在的意义，那梦逻辑又为何要将她创造出来呢？不过，文向她解释说，在幻想乡里，人类和妖怪本就可以和谐共生。大家都得学会以一种益于稳定的方式来疏导自己的欲求。艾科托塔并不必尽其所能地增殖病毒，而只需要与人类保持某种对立的关系就可以了。即使这种“对立”是以开展弹幕战或者是给予一些无伤大雅的惊吓这样安全无害的形式体现也无妨。或者，就像卡特琳娜的提议那样，警醒他们保持良好的健康习惯。卡特琳娜希望从小铃与她的禁忌知识的库藏中寻找一些能帮助艾科托塔平稳地融入幻想乡的生活的信息，而艾科托塔也承诺，她会尽力做一位好公民。</div></td></tr></tbody></table>
