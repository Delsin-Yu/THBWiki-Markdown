## 评论
1. Fairytale of Avarice

  
Ah, finally. A classical piece again! When did I uploaded the last one? For my 100 subscriber special right? Oh my. Time to change that!   

This one´s rather short, but it´s the "introductory" track for Tainted Heart, so it kind of fulfills its purpose.   

  

2. A day in the Marches

  
Good thing I´ve brought an umbrella with me.   

  

3. The Bits and Bytes of Seasons

  
It´s almost like a LAN party.   

  

4. A failed waltz for my Fragile, Little Sister

  
...and I even decorated the mansion after her preferences, but still...   

Wanted to try something. Use as few instrument as possible. This is the result. Strings, a celesta, a piano and a bowed glass. I kind of like it, especially the innocent, yet dissonant sounding beginning and the spacy outro. I guess I also got better at combining multiple tracks.&#160;:)   

Wonder if you can tell which track I used for the outro. :P   

  

5. Mistress Meiji´s Tea Time

  
Heavenly retrospective.   

  

6. the Open Fields of Avarice

  
Nazurin~~   

What? Another accordion arrange of Commander? How can I? D:   

These two things just fit so well together, A Tiny, Tiny Commander is one of the best stage 1 boss themes ZUN composed, at least in my opinion, alongside Mooned Insect. And the possibilities for Nazrin´s theme to be arranged are great, but I´ll stick to mah accordion. ;P    

I somewhat orientated myself to the soundtrack of Dark Cloud 2, but it didn´t turn out as expected. :x   

  

7. Temper cooled down

  
Sometimes, even she has to relax.   

  

8. Onigashima in Misery

  
Gathering for feasts. How exciting.   

  

9. Powerful forbidding

  
Forbidden by force.   

Everytime I listen to a fan-made theme related to Touhou, I wonder how these composers manage to capture the essence of ZUN´s music, letting it sound as an external composition by the latter mentioned.   

  

10. The Waltz in your town

  
Dancing with a friend... or just testing my new camera.   

I´ve fallen in love with this track since I heard a folk arrange of it on Nico. Too bad it only plays for two scenes.&#160;:(   

  

11. Ein kleine Mitternachtsmelodie

  
Futo, Futo, Feng-Shui!!   

There is no greater force than the might of Feng-Shui!   

  

12. Archivar aus alter Zeit

  
DJ wants you to adjust your audio!   

This theme seems to be rather non-climaxing in comparison to the previous themes (why is Border of Life so praised? -_-), but I like it more than a few others, like Nuclear Fusion or Inanimate Dream. Welp, I guess I´m weird. :P   

  

13. Dream of Magnolia

  
Dream of a withered beauty.   

  

14. Course of Merriment

  
Dawn, Day and Dusk. Combined in one.   

Three Fairies of Light, childishly playing the whole day, planning pranks and tricks, until they fall asleep under the gloomy moonlight shining down on the Forest of Magic.
