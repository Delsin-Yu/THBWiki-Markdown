## 评论
  
Idolatrize World immediately caught my attention when I first heard the Touhou 17 soundtrack! It's the definition of epic and the perfect theme for a character like Keiki!
I wanted to put as much time and effort into every detail of the cover and push my boundaries, knowing this won't be an easy one! I wanted to make a big jump with everything, whether it be the arrangement process, drums, guitar, orchestra and synths.
I think the most challenging part of making this remix was actually arranging it to preserve the epic essence of the original. This is definitely one of my favorite compositions from ZUN! Let me know what you think!
Thanks for listening! ^^
  
  
There's two references in the solo! Mayumi's Theme (also inspired by 暁Records HANIPAGANDA) and Jelly Stone (Eika Ebisu's Theme) to reference the Touhou 17 Metal Medley I did beforehand!
Hope you guys enjoy the fitting, goofy references! What do you think?&#160;:)
