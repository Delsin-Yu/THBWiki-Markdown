## 评论
  
I played Chimata's Theme from Touhou UM on guitar! The theme is very similar to Idolatrize World, so I mixed up both songs to create one super boss theme! There's also Fortunate Kitten (Mike's Theme) thrown in there as well. This video is a 2 year anniversary since I've released my cover of Idolatrize World, it's all coming together haha!
  
  
Tenkyuu for listening! ^^
