<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2d&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A rather peculiar human from the outside world.<br>Praised by her peers and mentors, she was a top notch Yosakoi Dancer who spent many years of dedicated training to reach her current level.<br><br>Her favorite hobby was to sneak away into quiet areas like forests in order to practice her trade.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">来自外界的一个十分特殊的人类。<br>通过积年累月的练习，她成为了一名顶尖的夜来舞舞者，受到同伴和师傅的交口称赞。<br><br>她最大的嗜好就是偷偷溜进森林之类僻静的地方，训练自己的技艺。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2d&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">On one of her practice treks, she accidently crossed the Hakurei Barrier and into Gensokyo during a time when it was heavily weakened (as a result of the events of The Shattered Sky).<br>While she was lost, she stumbled upon a youkai who brutally stripped her of all autonomy. <br><br>Who was this youkai? ### 东方催狐谭EX

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭EX-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2dEX&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Kagemo had been possessed by a mysterious mastermind who permanently attached a white fox mask onto her face. <br>The mask itself was cursed to constantly seep demonic energy into her system. Should anyone dare to touch it and take it off her face (not that Kagemo had the willpower to take it off herself), the masks's energy would enter the victim's system through their fingers and cause some horrific cognitive mutations.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">如上所述，影面受到谜之黑幕的操纵。戴在她脸上的白色狐面，浸透着诅咒的力量。<br>被剥夺自由的她本人自不必说，即使有人想把面具剥离，诅咒也会经接触者的指尖传导过去，严重侵蚀那人的精神。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭EX-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2dEX&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">"Kagemo" isn't her name, it's a measly nickname given to her by her master. They never bothered to learn her name, there was no point; after all, the human side of her didn't exist anymore. <br>As she was a regular girl, Kagemo was unable to use magic until the mask was put on her. The mask also served as a power source, drawing some of the master's magic. <br>Even though her bullets look like colorful candy, the amount of evil contained in a single bullet can turn the nicest person into a mindless beast controlled by the mastermind. <br>Kagemo, however, still retained a bit of what she used to be.  She is still a cheerful young lady who enjoys dancing her worries away.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">“影面”并非她的本名。为了抹消其作为人类的性格，有必要抹消其本名，故主人给她起了这个绰号。<br>在身为普通人类之时完全不会运用妖术等，由于面具中注入了主人的魔力，戴上之后变得可以使用弹幕来攻击了。<br>虽然她的弹幕看上去如糖果般光彩夺目，但实际上每一颗光弹中都包含强烈的诅咒，足以把最善良温和的人变成任由黑幕操纵的凶恶人偶。<br>其实她还保留了一点原来的性格，即使遭人操纵，她欢快的一面并没有改变。她依然是一位会用欢乐的舞蹈驱散烦恼的、开朗阳光的女孩，这一点始终不变。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭EX-3" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2dEX&quot;,3&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">A grand plan was in order - the mastermind, still in her spirit form, would wish for a new body with a hinnagami. Kagemo was tasked to gather dirt from unsecured graves for the creation of this wishing doll. <br>Kagemo had to hasten unless she wanted to be punished by her mistress. Thus, Meimei was born after just a few weeks. Regular hinnagami take years to create, so Meimei clearly wasn't fit for the enormous wish she was created for. The mastermind was extremely disappointed and decided to discard her immediately, but another idea sprung into their mind.<br>What if Kagemo used Meimei to track down the special wish-fulfilling jewel that Inari has? Meimei proved to be useful after all... well, as a GPS more than anything. <br>After using the jewel, the mastermind created a new body for herself - a voluptuous, ill-wishing kitsune lady that named herself Shirigai no Mamo. <br>To her, Meimei and Kagemo had zero worth, so to avoid being blamed for the theft of the jewel, she sent the duo back to the Harvest Moon Shrine. <br>If they were caught, they would die, not her. She's clearly not the nicest person to her underlings...</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">处于灵体状态的黑幕构思了计划，打算利用人形神获得新的肉身。影面负责收集无人参拜的枯冢上的泥土，用它来制作人偶。<br>要是不抓紧的话影面会受到严厉的处罚，于是几周后梅美诞生了。然而通常情况下人偶需要花费几年才能制成，所以梅美没有足够的力量实现主人的愿望，这倒也在情理之中。<br>大失所望的主谋本打算将梅美抛弃，但峰回路转，她有了一个新的计划。<br>让梅美发挥的力量，帮助影面寻找稻荷神持有的如意宝珠，这样如何呢？<br>结果证明，梅美作为…向导非常有用。<br>使用了宝珠之后，作为黑幕的恶狐获得了一具美艳的肉身。她给自己取了一个新的名字，唤作“纣真藻”。<br>当宝珠失窃被察觉时，黑幕担心遭到命妇的追捕，于是命令影面与梅美将宝珠还回名月神社。<br>对于她来说，部下只是工具而已。哪怕两人遭到逮捕，甚至被杀，也和黑幕她自己没有任何关系。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方催狐谭EX-4" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u50ac\u72d0\u8c2dEX&quot;,4&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">During the time of running around Gensokyo, being chased by Inari's servants or the heroines, Kagemo and Meimei had a lot of time to bond. <br>Back in the village, Kagemo hid Meimei to prevent her from attracting the eyes of others, but Meimei couldn't stand constantly running away from everybody. <br>They deeply care about each other, having built up a younger sister- elder sister relationship thanks to their similar fates.. Kagemo treated Meimei not like a fellow servant, but like a best friend. <br>Considering how her autonomy was taken from her, the wishing doll was actually the only glimmer of hope for Kagemo's sanity.  <br>Thanks to Meimei and all the fun they have on a daily basis, Kagemo hasn't lost the entirety of her mind thanks to Meimei. <br>Not yet, at least... Mamo has taken note of this, so she will "toy" with her to get rid of the only trace of the girl Kagemo once used to be.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">当受到命妇和自机们的追捕，不得不在幻想乡东躲西藏的时候，影面与梅美的羁绊加深了。<br>在村子里，为了不引人注目，影面不得不将梅美隐藏起来，但是梅美厌倦了逃避众人的目光。<br>影面不是像对待隶属同一主人的同僚，而是像对待朋友那样对待梅美。不知不觉，梅美把影面看作了姐姐那般的存在，她自己也成为了影面的妹妹那样的存在。<br>因为有着共同的不幸境遇，所以互相珍惜着彼此。<br>实际上对于被剥夺了自由的影面来说，梅美是让她保持清醒的重要因素。<br>两人每天一起玩耍，其中蕴含着回归原本的自己的希望。<br>但其实……幕后黑手也注意到了这一点。也许她已经在考虑，为了彻底抹除原人格，进一步折磨影面的精神吧。<br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
