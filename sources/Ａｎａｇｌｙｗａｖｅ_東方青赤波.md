## 评论

  
東方×Didgeridoo×vaporwave  

サークル"ξ"（グザイ）より委託の摩訶不思議東方アレンジ！  

カセットテープでの頒布。3Dメガネ付き！  

近日中にbandcampにてボーナストラック以外全曲フリー公開予定。
  


___


  
"ξ" is a didgeridoo player, and also the project`s name of touhou didgeridoo arrangement. "3D -DELIGHT DIDGERIDOO DAYZ-", his first work, has been released in late 2015. In 2020, he turned into one of touhou vaporwave arrangers, and released "Are you sure you want to delete all feelings?". It was a beginning of "didgeriwave", a vaporwave new sub-genre.  

Now, the anaglyph-3D-vaporwave has arrived.  

"Ａｎａｇｌｙｗａｖｅ" pulled chopped &amp; screwed samples from "3D".
