### 新版设定
#### 一面设定

<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="一面设定-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e00\u9762\u8bbe\u5b9a&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Curious and Imaginative Wani<br><br>鰐鳥 海美容（わにとり うみびよ）<br>Umibiyo Wanitori<br><br>Species：Wani<br>Ability：Manipulating bodies of water<br><br>If that other wani back from the Sea Rain Incident could be said to, at least outwardly, represent the more professional side of the wani, then Umibiyo can be said to represent the more simplistic, hedonistic side of the wani.<br><br>A rather innocent girl, Umibiyo was rather weak before the incident, being unable to stand-up to even the ice fairy. However, she wound up being one of the many people who got powered up from the incident, putting her on par with the likes of the aforementioned fellow wani from the Sea Rain incident.<br><br>That being said, she's not able to use her newfound power effectively, largely due to not being used to being so powerful, along with the fact that she's a simple-minded individual in general.<br><br>She spent most of her life dwelling underwater in Misty Lake, out of fear of someone challenging her and being unable to stand-up to them.<br>With her new power, she tried leaving the lake, with newfound confidence that she could stand up to anyone she faces.<br><br>Her first thought was to try going to the Human Village, out of curiosity as to what these so-called "hue mans" were like.<br>However, she was foolish enough to try asking the Hakurei shrine maiden for directions, which is what led to the two fighting.<br><br>Despite this, she still tried looking for the Human Village after the shrine maiden was far enough away, this time feeling like she could just find it by herself.<br><br>In a way, she's rather stubborn when she gets determined.<br></div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem"></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
