## 评论

  
The fourth and the last in the line of Touhou Arrangement Projects, featuring 20 tracks from the Touhou franchise. Guest arrangers include blowitch, Yiter and konamiscc.  

  

Well, it's finally here! The fourth and final Touhou arrangement album! It's been quite a ride. With this, I think me and the others have arranged pretty much all the tracks we wanted to hear in an FM style (and - of course more precisely - Yousuke Yasui-like FM style).  

I myself think that I'm probably done with this specific style of FM for a while - there are many other composers out there who are honestly doing a way better job at this than me (shoutout to the guest artists on this album and Rumya from the previous album). I leave it up to you for the future. For now, enjoy this release!
