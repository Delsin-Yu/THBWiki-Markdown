Kiseki was perfectly fine with undoing all the work she'd done; her only desire was to make Aimi's desires a reality. Sanae pointed out that Aimi very well could have chosen to mistreat someone so totally devoted to her, but as far as Sanae had seen, Aimi had always treated Kiseki with respect as a person and not merely a tool.
