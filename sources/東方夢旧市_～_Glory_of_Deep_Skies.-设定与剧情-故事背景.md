<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="=-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;=&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">---The Hakurei Shrine, in the chill of autumn.<br><br>The Shrine Maiden is sitting on the shrine front, watching <br>the evening sky as it bursts into many beautiful colors. <br><br>While this scene seems to be a Romantic poet’s dream, this<br>scenario spells disaster for her.<br><br>Reimu: “The sky has been looking weird for the past few days.<br>I've got a bad feeling about this.<br>Has there been anything unusual going on?”<br><br>Behind her, there stood a tengu reporter, Aya Shameimaru.<br><br>Aya: “The sky has been overflowing with large amounts of magical<br>power. Seems like it affects all of Gensokyo at the moment. <br>Tengu patrols have been investigating it for a while; we got nothing,<br>though. What do you think about this?"<br><br>Reimu: “I don't know. I hope it's just a natural occurrence, and<br>I hope it ends soon. Things have been getting stranger by the hour.”<br><br>Aya jots some things down on a notepad.<br><br>Aya: “Oh, right! Ms. Reimu, there has been a report about a recent<br>attack on the Human Village last night. Our tengu patrol said there<br>was a strange light, like some kind of overwhelming danmaku battle,<br>but it caused physical damage to buildings and property; luckily,<br>nobody got hurt. We tried to fight back, but the thing - whatever<br>it is - is too strong! It managed to knock everyone out and escape."<br><br>Reimu turns back to Aya.<br><br>Reimu: “Why didn't you tell me this earlier? This sounds very fishy<br>to me. All of these things happening at once couldn’t be a coincidence.<br>I have to do something.”<br><br>Reimu knows the drill - very well. Something unusual happens;<br>and then there are either visitors (whether they present themselves<br>as such or not) or local residents who become strong enough to cause<br>trouble.<br><br>Although, “unusual” has been becoming a less effective word<br>to use in these incidents, as they are called, since they’ve been<br>occurring much more frequently; it’s difficult to tell what’s “usual”<br>around here anymore.<br><br>Flying into the scene comes Marisa - a totally normal magician.<br>Ever since the unusual light shows have started occurring - rainbows,<br>auroras, phenomena that probably haven’t been named yet -<br>she’s been “researching” them.<br><br>Aya, not wanting to bother the Shrine Maiden from her work any<br>longer, flies off to find another witness to interview.<br><br>Marisa: “Hey, Reimu, are you seein’ this? There’s crazy stuff happening<br>everywhere! I think my magic’s goin’ out of control a bit too-”<br><br>Reimu: “Good, you’re just who I wanted to see.”<br><br>Marisa: “Huh? Don’t tell me we’re goin’ crazy-people-shooting again<br>already. Didn’t we just give that door person the boot?”<br><br>Reimu: “Don’t you think I would like a break, too? Come on, let’s get<br>this over with already.”<br><br>And the two of them are off, each going their own way, leaving the<br>shrine grounds empty. Due to the new barrier’s influence on magic power,<br>the forest simply radiates with it. Night is approaching fast, and even so,<br>the strange light persists.<br><br>-- However, in these dark hours, many monsters and youkai awaken. <br>With this new barrier in effect, Gensokyo is in complete danger; <br>the enhanced magic power has turned the land of fantasy into a designated<br>battleground.<br><br>Somewhere, unknown to the heroines. "She" is standing there,<br>gazing at the majestic view of Gensokyo's sunset.<br>In her hand, there lies a silver key.<br>She exhales deeply, waves the key, and the portal closes shut.<br><br>"This will be a long, chaotic night."--</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">——寒冷深秋的博丽神社<br><br>巫女坐在神社前，观赏着在夜空中绽放的绚丽的色彩。<br><br>尽管这是浪漫诗人的梦想，但在她眼里这预示着灾难。<br><br>灵梦：“过去几日的天空看起来很奇怪。<br>我有种不祥的预感。<br>有什么不寻常的事发生了吗？”<br><br>在她身后，站着一位天狗记者，射命丸文。<br><br>文：“天空中积蓄了大量的魔力，现在看来它已经影响了整个幻想乡。<br>尽管天狗侦察部队已经前往调查，但她们一无所获。<br>你怎么看？”<br><br>灵梦：“不知道，希望这只是一种自然现象，最好它早点结束。看起来事情正变得越来越奇怪了。”<br><br>文在笔记本上记下了什么。<br><br>文：“对了！灵梦，我这里有一篇报道，关于昨夜发生在人类村落里的一场袭击。天狗调查部队说她们看见了一束奇怪的光，就像是弹幕的攻击。它对建筑和财产造成了危害，所幸没有人员伤亡。我们试图反击，但是它——太强了！它击倒了众人然后逃之夭夭。”<br><br>灵梦转头看向文。<br><br>灵梦：“你怎么不早说？听上去很可疑嘛。这些事情一起发生绝不是巧合，我得做些什么。”<br><br>灵梦对于如何应对突发状况了如指掌。有外来的访客进入了幻想乡（无论她们是否有什么目的），或是这里的居民变得过于强大以至于引起了麻烦。<br><br>实际上，由于在诸多异变中频繁发生，“不寻常”这个词已经不再适用了，对于这里的每个人来说，很难讲什么才是“平常”。<br><br>这时，普通的魔法使——魔理沙飞来了。<br>自从那其异的光发生后，魔理沙一直在“研究”它——彩虹？极光？或是别的什么未名现象。<br><br>文并不想过多打扰巫女的工作，飞去采访其他目击者了。<br><br>魔理沙：“嘿，灵梦，你看到了吗？到处都有疯狂的事在发生！我感觉自己的魔法也在失控的边缘——”<br><br>灵梦：“很好，我正要去找你呢。”<br><br>魔理沙：“嗯？别跟我说又要去和那个狂人对战。我们不是已经被那个操纵门的家伙<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">1</a></sup>踢走了吗？”<br><br>灵梦：“你以为我就不想休息吗？来吧，我们赶紧把这事解决了。”<br><br>她们各自出发了，留下一个空空荡荡的神社。在新的结界的作用下，森林受到了其影响。很快夜幕降临，即便如此，奇怪的光芒依旧存在。<br><br>在这漆黑的时刻，无数的怪物和妖怪醒来了。<br>在那个新的结界的作用下，幻想乡处在了危险的边缘。不断增幅的魔力正在把这片幻想的土地变成命中注定的战场。<br><br>在某个主人公们不知道的地方，“她”正站在那里，<br>眺望着幻想乡壮丽的落日景象。<br>她手中拿着一把银钥匙<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">2</a></sup>。<br>她深吸一口气，摇动了银钥匙，入口便关上了。<br><br>“看来，这将是个漫长而混沌的夜晚。”——<br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
