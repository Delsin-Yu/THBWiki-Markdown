class="tt-header" id="=-143" data-pos="&#91;&quot;=&quot;,143&#93;"><td colspan="2" id="" class="tt-header" lang="zh"><div class="poem"></div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="=-144" data-pos="&#91;&quot;=&quot;,144&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">　――そこから『宇佐見菫子失踪実験　対策本部』は『<ruby lang="ja"><rb>宇佐見菫子</rb><rp> (</rp><rt>ドッペルゲンガー</rt><rp>) </rp></ruby>营救总部”，继续进行作战会议。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="=-145" data-pos="&#91;&quot;=&quot;,145&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div id="=-148" data-pos="&#91;&quot;=&quot;,148&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">　霊夢はもどかしそうに言った。</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">　灵梦不耐烦地说。</div></td></tr><tr
