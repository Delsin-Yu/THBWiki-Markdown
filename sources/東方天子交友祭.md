## 评论
  
A simple story about friendship between two person who just met. Sometimes you don’t need words to appreciate the joys of friendship. Featuring Suika and Tenshi from IAMP and Hisouten! so this makes it a doujin of a doujin game from a doujin game?! Also, a set of postcards, each one depicting the relationship between your favorite Touhou characters. Showcasing the works of various CDS artists, it is the perfect set to go with the doujin.
