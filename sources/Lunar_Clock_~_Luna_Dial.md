## 评论
  
I played Sakuya's Theme from Touhou EoSD on guitar! I also added Septette for the Dead Princess (Remilia's Theme) in the second half of the song. Scarlet Devil Mansion is jamming tonight!
  
  
My goal was to rewrite the song with a strict structure in mind and make each section unique with its own instrumentation. There's a lot of ambient, synth and orchestral sound design happening in the background. I hope you like the riff I wrote too.
  
  
Thank you for listening. Let me know what you think!
