<table><tbody><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-1" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,1&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">Legends tell of sprites and youkai that wander and lurk in the ocean depths. <br>Some are benevolent, some are not.<br><br>Iroha is benevolent.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">传说中，有各式的灵体与妖怪在海洋的深处漫游潜伏，伺机而动。<br>它们中的一部分是友好的，而另一部分不是。<br><br>而彩波则属于前者。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-2" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,2&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">She has a cheerful disposition, though being strangers, the protagonists see little of it. <br>Her clumsy and delicate nature doesn't help that matter.<br>Her cheer is a (well-meaning) ploy to fight off her anxieties of being eaten or something of the sort. They're not quite unfounded.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">她被认为是性格开朗的，虽然有作为陌生人，我们的主角并不能全面认识她的原因。<br>她笨拙而细腻的天性并不能在这件事上起到任何作用。<br>她的乐观或许也只是一种用来战胜她对于被吃掉或者其他这类糟糕事情的（善意的）策略罢了。而这并非毫无根据。</div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-3" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,3&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">She's rather wise for her age, though her anxieties typically prevent her from sharing her wisdom. <br>Her preferred method of communication are her fire dragons. These typically scare off possible offenders. <br>Being a product of her ability, however, they're simply illusions. She's well-practiced, nonetheless.<br><br>As an Amabie, she's able to sing beautiful songs of prophecy.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">就她的年龄来说，她相当聪明，尽管她的不安使得她无法与其他人分享她的智慧。<br>她更倾向于选择的交流方法是她的火龙。这可以有效地震慑所有潜在的威胁。<br>作为她能力的产物，这些火龙实际上只是幻象而已。尽管如此，她也已经习惯了使用这种手段。<br><br>作为一位尼彦，她能够唱出美妙的预言之歌。<br></div></td></tr><tr class="tt-content" id="东方潮圣书-4" data-pos="&#91;&quot;\u4e1c\u65b9\u6f6e\u5723\u4e66&quot;,4&#93;"><td class="tt-ja" lang="ja"><div class="poem">She’s actually closely associated with the Kazarashi household.<br>Like most, she finds their resident shrine maiden rather... unsettling.</div></td><td class="tt-zh" lang="zh"><div class="poem">实际上，她与风岚家的关系十分密切。<br>与大多数人一样，她对突入了苍穹洋的巫女感到十分的不安。<br></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
