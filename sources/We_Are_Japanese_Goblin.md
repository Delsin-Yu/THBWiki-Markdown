## 评论
  
I made a cover of We Are Japanese Goblin! This is my first time singing! It has been months of hard work practicing vocals, Japanese and learning video editing. I've also referenced Bad Apple!! in this song. Thank you so much for your patience, as I was putting this together. It's been an exciting challenge!
  
  
Shout-out to MTB for inspiring this project with his amazing animations, and providing screencaps of the MMD maps. You're awesome, bro!
