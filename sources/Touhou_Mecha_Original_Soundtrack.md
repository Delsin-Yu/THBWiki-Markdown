## 评论
```
This is my first release in over a year! I was fortunate enough to get in contact with the guys at Dai-Sukima Dan [[1]](http://dsd.faceroll.net) and work on this visual novel with GraphyteRonin, who did most of the artwork and programming. It was incredibly inspiring for me since Graph is SUPER TALENTED and his work within the Ren'py engine is definitely incredible. These songs have been in the works for a while now and I promised I'd release the soundtrack concurrently with the release of chapter 2 of the novel, which once again you can get at dsd.faceroll.net. He had another musician previously, Flamezero16, who due to various reasons wasn't able to work on the soundtrack after he made two rockin' battle themes, which I've also included in this album.

Of course, if you've been following me recently, you know I've been getting really heavy into the Touhou fandom, and this is one of the ways I am able to contribute, through my music! However, since it is all derivative work, I am keeping this album free to download. If you want to support my original music, my other albums are available for pay-what-you-want on this site, and any amounts are appreciated, so I can afford to eat and all that.

The visual novel itself is about youkai [demons] embroiled in a civil war underground. The Touhou games all have a folk/fantasy setting, but Graph took that setting and threw in giant robots, guns and missiles. As of posting this soundtrack, we're currently at chapter 2 of the story, with more chapters sure to come. More music, too.
```
