## 评论

  
A collection of three short comics.  

  

In Fall Ritual, the twin goddesses of autumn have just produced the season's first wine, and are just waiting for the newspaper reviewer, when things get out of hand...  

  

In To Hell With Jealousy!, underground bridge guardian Parsee is lamenting her bitter fate as always, when she gets an unexpected visit that will change her life... if only for a day.  

  

In Once Upon A Time In Eternity, exiled Moon princess Kaguya has a grand plan for an expo of moon paraphernalia, she'll just need someone to advertise it...  

  

___

  
ストーリーを三つあります。  

  

文は秋姉妹の新しいワインを味わう。  

  

パルスィはヘーロインに会って、旧都を探す。  

  

輝夜は月大会を催したくて、ヘーロインに連絡する。
