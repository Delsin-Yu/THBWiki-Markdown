## 评论
  
Here's my metal cover of Utsuho Reiuji's theme from Touhou SA (Stage 6 Boss).
  
  
I have also referenced Cirno's Theme (inspired by Memories of Phantasm) and Orin's Theme, because they hang out together!
  
  
My goal with this cover was to add many of my own ideas while still staying true to the original composition. Nuclear Fusion is an absolute banger and an iconic Touhou classic too. I hope you enjoy my take on it!
  
  
Thank you for listening!
