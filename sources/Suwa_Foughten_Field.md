## 评论
  
I made a cover of Kanako's Theme from Touhou MoF. As a bonus, I played Suwako's Theme (Native Faith) - of course, wearing her hat in the music video as well!
  
  
I really wanted to go all out with heavy riffs and hard smacking vibes throughout the whole song, with the two calm bridges contrasting the arrangement. I hope you enjoy my take on this 2hu classic - it's been an absolute blast making it.
  
  
Thank you very much for listening and for your ongoing support!
